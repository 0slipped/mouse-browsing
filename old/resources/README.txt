; Hotkey legend: . . .

; . . . ^
	represents holding the CTRL key down.
; . . . +
	represents holding the SHIFT key down.
	
; . . . Xbutton 1.
	the back mouse button.
; . . . XButton2.
	the front mouse button.
	
; . . . MButton.
	the middle mouse button (click).
; . . . LButton.
	left click.
; . . . RButton.
	right click.

; ==================================================

; All hotkeys/functions: . . .

; . . . SHIFT + F1
	(accessability button)

; . . . CTRL (+ SHIFT) + TAB 
	(next/previous tab).
	
; . . . CTRL + W or T 
	(open/close a tab).

; . . . PGUP or PGDN 
	(go up/down on a page).

; . . . Minimize or Maximize
	(active windows).

; . . . CTRL + '+' or '-' 
	(zoom in/out page).

; . . . HOME or END 
	(go to start/end).
	
; . . . F11
	(fullscreen mode).
